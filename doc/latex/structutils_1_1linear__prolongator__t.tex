The prolongation operator acts on the coarse data as follows

\[ U^l_{i_c,j_c,k_c} + \sum_{i_d=1}^{N_d} \Delta\bar{U} \frac{x^{i_d, l+1}_{i_f,j_f,k_f}-x^{i_d, l}_{i_c,j_c,k_c} }{\Delta x^(i_d,l)} \left( 1 - \frac{ V^{l+1}_{i_f,j_f,k_f} }{ \sum V^{l+1}_{i^\prime_f,j^\prime_f,k^\prime_f} } \right) \] Where $(i_f,j_f,k_f)$ are the indices of the children of cell $(i_c,j_c,k_c)$ and the partial volume sum extends over the two fine cells along direction $i_d$. The limited slope $\Delta\bar{U}$ is computed with the limiter given by {\ttfamily LimT}. \mbox{\Hypertarget{structutils_1_1linear__prolongator__t_a594f4255a1addcf468d090432f02f500}\label{structutils_1_1linear__prolongator__t_a594f4255a1addcf468d090432f02f500}}
